Learn More About Us





       Welcome to Pushkaraj Classes, a leading provider of educational coaching services. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to achieve their academic and personal goals.

     Our team of highly qualified and experienced educators is dedicated to helping students succeed. We offer a wide range of coaching classes for students from 8th to 10th standard (in English, Semi-English, and Marathi medium), as well as FYJC to SYJC Commerce Maharashtra Board and CA Foundation in all subjects. Our classes are designed to be engaging, interactive, and personalized to meet the unique needs of each student.

     At Pushkaraj Classes, we believe that education is the key to unlocking a brighter future. We are committed to providing high-quality coaching services that are accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment where all students can thrive.

      Our students are our top priority, and we are always looking for ways to improve our services and exceed their expectations. Whether you are looking to improve your grades, prepare for an exam, or enhance your career prospects, we are here to help you achieve your goals.

     Thank you for considering Pushkaraj Classes as your educational partner. We look forward to helping you achieve success!
